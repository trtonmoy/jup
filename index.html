Ask Quick Question

MixrBot is a Bot that allows users to buy numbers that they can use to set new telegram accounts. The payment is done with ETH and is paid through the bot wallet.

In order to get a new number, it is necessary first to buy a ticket. This is like a receipt that can be redeemed for a phone number right after or whenever you want. This purchase is done with ETH and costs 0.003 ETH.

Once this is done, your user will have a ticket that can be redeemed by clicking on "Get Number". Before doing this, we recommend to interact to our bot from a Telegram Desktop so you can use your phone app to add the new telegram account with the provided number. If you don't do so, you might lose the step by step telegram account creation when getting back to our bot commands.

"Get Number" will give you a number that you can introduce in Telegram APP from your phone device. Once this is done, you will be sent an SMS to that number. The bot will get it so you just need to click on "Get SMS" and it will be provided. Enter that SMS on your phone app and the account will be created.

"Cancel Number" is a button that is useful in case the phone number you are given is unavailable or banned in Telegram, then you can cancel it and ask for another one using "Get Number" again. Your ticket will only be redeemed once the SMS is sent to you through the bot.

MixrBot allows you to create a wallet from scratch or import an existing one. If you make a new one, you can then send eth from your metamask or any other wallet to the new one and the funds will appear shortly. You can also withdraw that ETH by sending back to your wallet or to any other person at any time.
